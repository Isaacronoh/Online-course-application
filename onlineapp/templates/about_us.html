About Us

Wajir East TVC is a public technical vocational college situated 5KM along Mandera road in Wagberi Sub-location, Wagberi location, Wajir East Sub-county, Wajir County. The college was started by the then world fund committee as a community project and later on the National government came in.

Our Vision

Our Mission

To provide Technical, Industrial, Entrepreneurial Training that promotes innovation and creativity to meet global socio-economic and environmental challenges.
